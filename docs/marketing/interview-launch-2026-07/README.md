experience. The campaign focuses on realistic role, company, resume, and job
description based mock interviews in Hindi and English.

## Audience

- Location: India
- Age: 18-34
- Languages: Hindi and English
- Freshers and final-year students
- Campus placement candidates
- Early-career job switchers
- Non-technical and technical job seekers

Create separate ad groups for campus placements, job switchers, and
role-specific interview preparation. Do not combine every audience in one group.

## Controlled Test Budget

- Instagram Reels: INR 500/day for 3 days
- YouTube Shorts: INR 300/day for 3 days
- LinkedIn: publish organically first; use paid promotion after registration
  conversion is verified
- WhatsApp: Status and direct sharing first

This is a test budget, not a traffic or conversion guarantee. Pause an ad when
it receives clicks but produces no interview starts.

## Measure

Track these events separately:

1. Landing page viewed
2. Registration started
3. Registration completed
4. Interview setup completed
5. Interview started

The main success metric is cost per interview started, not likes or video views.
